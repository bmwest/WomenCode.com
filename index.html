“More than half of professional occupations in U.S. are held by women (57%). But the percentage of computing occupations held by women lags far behind. 26% of computing occupations are held by women, 3% by African-American women, 5% by Asian women, 2% by Hispanic/Latina women.”
--ncwit.org
